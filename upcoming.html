Event Highlights

  • May 9, 2026
  • 6:00 PM – 7:00 PM
  • Online (X Space)

Join us for an engaging and life-saving conversation on Sickle Cell Crisis: What to Do and What Not to Do. This live X (Twitter) Space brings together experienced medical professionals who will break down the realities of sickle cell crises, share practical steps for emergency response, and correct common misconceptions that put lives at risk. Whether you are living with sickle cell, a caregiver, or simply want to be informed, this session will equip you with the knowledge to respond confidently and make better health decisions. Don’t miss this opportunity to learn, ask questions, and be part of a movement that promotes awareness and saves lives.
